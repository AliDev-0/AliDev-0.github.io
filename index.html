About me

🎬 Film Producer 💻 Full-Stack Dev 📍 Kurdistan, Iran 🎂 Jan 26, 2005 🏷 AliDev.0
"Cinema tells stories. Code builds worlds. I do both."

Ali Moradi Dev (legal name: Ali Moradi) was born on January 26, 2005 in Bijar, Kurdistan Province, Iran. From an early age he worked at the intersection of storytelling and technology, developing a dual career rare in either industry.

In Iranian cinema, he has contributed to notable productions as producer assistant, co-producer, and associate producer — officially listed on IMDb (nm17111758). Simultaneously, he built a full-stack development practice focused on open-source tools that bridge the film and tech sectors.

Known online as AliDev.0, his work has been featured on IssueWire, Crunchbase, and dev.to, earning international recognition for an approach to creative technology that is distinctly Iranian in perspective and globally relevant in execution.
