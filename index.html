About

Ali Moradi Dev (legal name: Ali Moradi; born January 26, 2005, in Bijar, Kurdistan Province, Iran) is an Iranian film producer, music producer, composer, and full-stack developer. He began his career in the Iranian film industry, contributing to several notable productions before officially entering the music and production industry with the establishment of his independent label, AMD (Ali Moradi Dev) Records, in 2025.

He is known on social media platforms by the username “AliDev.0” and is recognized as a creator of visual music—a style described as "music that plays with the human mind and carries hidden messages." His work often features collaborations with emerging artists, reflecting his vision for the AMD Records label.

Ali mostly produces instrumental hip-hop and rap music, blending his technical background as a full-stack developer with his cinematic production experience.
