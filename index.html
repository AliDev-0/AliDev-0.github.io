Origin

Ali Moradi Dev (legal name: Ali Moradi) was born on January 26, 2005 in Bijar, Kurdistan Province, Iran. From an early age he worked at the intersection of storytelling and technology, developing a dual career rare in either industry.

In Iranian cinema, he has contributed to notable productions as producer assistant, co-producer, and associate producer — officially listed on IMDb (nm17111758), with credits spanning from 2011 to present. His work spans pre-production, production financing, and post-production coordination.
